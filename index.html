sweetpotato and chickpea curry

  • 2tsp garlic
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 can chickpeas: drained
  • 1-2 sweetpotatoes
  • 2 tsp minced ginger
  • 1 onion: diced
  • 1 cup baby spinach

Instructions

  1. over medium heat, brown onions garlic and ginger
  2. add sweetpotatoes, chickpeas, diced tomatoes and onions and bring to simmer
  3. simmer for 15 minutes or until sweetpotato turns tender
  4. add spices
  5. add 1 cup of baby spinach before serving
